# What is BlankFX?

BlankFX is a foreign exchange protocol built on Ethereum. It lets you swap between stablecoins (USDC, USDT, RLUSD, EURC) at oracle-anchored rates with instant on-chain settlement.

## How it differs from other DEXs

Most decentralized exchanges treat currency pairs like any other token swap. You get an AMM price based on pool ratios, which drifts from real FX rates depending on liquidity depth and arbitrage speed.

BlankFX anchors pricing to real-time oracle rates. The spread you see is the spread you get. No slippage surprises, no MEV extraction on your trade.

## Who it's for

- **Corporate treasuries** moving funds between USD and EUR stablecoins
- **Payment platforms** that need reliable FX execution behind their product
- **Stablecoin holders** who want to earn yield by providing liquidity
