# AML/CFT Policy

<Info>
The full BlankFX Anti-Money Laundering and Counter-Terrorist Financing Policy is maintained as a separate document and will be published here upon completion of the current review cycle.
</Info>

## Policy summary

BlankFX is committed to preventing the use of its protocol for money laundering, terrorist financing, or other illicit financial activity. This policy applies to all users, liquidity providers, market makers, and integration partners.

### Key principles

- **Know Your Customer (KYC)** — Users must be verified before accessing the protocol. Verification tiers determine available functionality and trade limits.
- **Transaction monitoring** — Automated monitoring of on-chain activity for suspicious patterns, including unusual trade sizes, rapid deposits/withdrawals, and interactions with flagged addresses.
- **Sanctions screening** — All wallet addresses are screened against OFAC, EU, and UN sanctions lists before trade execution.
- **Suspicious Activity Reporting** — Identified suspicious activity is reported to relevant authorities in accordance with applicable law.
- **Record keeping** — Transaction records are maintained for the period required by applicable regulation (minimum 5 years).

### Access tiers

| Tier | Verification | Limits |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Unverified | Wallet connection only | No trading access |
| Basic | Email + wallet verification | Limited trade size |
| Standard | KYC (ID + proof of address) | Standard trade limits |
| Institutional | Enhanced due diligence | Custom limits |

### Prohibited activities

The following activities are prohibited on BlankFX:
- Use of the protocol to launder proceeds of crime
- Transactions designed to evade sanctions
- Structuring transactions to avoid reporting thresholds
- Use of mixing services or privacy protocols to obscure fund origins before interacting with BlankFX
- Any activity that violates applicable anti-money laundering or counter-terrorist financing laws

### Compliance contact

For compliance inquiries, suspicious activity reports, or law enforcement requests, contact the BlankFX compliance team.
