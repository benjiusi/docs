# LP guide — how to earn

## Why provide liquidity?

BlankFX needs stablecoin liquidity to execute FX swaps. When you deposit into a pool, you earn a share of every swap fee generated by trades touching that pool.

The USD Pool is particularly attractive: because every cross-currency swap routes through it, USD Pool LPs earn from all trading activity on the protocol — not just USD-to-USD swaps.

## What you earn

Yield comes from trading fees. Every swap generates a spread fee (0.05-0.30% depending on the pair), and 100% of that fee accrues to the pool.

Your share of fees is proportional to your share of the pool. If you hold 10% of the pool's receipt tokens, you earn 10% of fees.

**There are no token emissions or inflationary rewards.** Yield is real revenue from real trades. This means yield scales with trading volume — more swaps, more fees, more yield.

## Step-by-step: depositing

### 1. Go to the Earn page

Navigate to [blankfx.org](https://blankfx.org) and click **Earn** in the navigation.

### 2. Choose a pool

| Pool | Accepts | Earns from |
|------|---------|------------|
| USD Pool | USDC, USDT, RLUSD | All trades (USD swaps + all cross-currency) |
| EUR Pool | EURC | EUR-related trades only |

**Recommendation for new LPs:** Start with the USD Pool. It earns from all trading pairs and accepts multiple tokens.

### 3. Click Supply and select your token

Pick which stablecoin you want to deposit. You only need one token — no pairing required.

### 4. Enter amount and approve

If this is your first deposit with that token, you'll need to approve the token spend (one-time transaction). Then confirm the deposit.

### 5. Receive receipt tokens

You'll receive pool receipt tokens (e.g. BFX-USD) representing your share of the pool. Hold these — they're your claim on the pool plus accumulated fees.

## How yield accrues

Your receipt token balance stays fixed, but the exchange rate between receipt tokens and the underlying stablecoin increases over time as fees accumulate.

```
Day 1:   1 BFX-USD = 1.0000 USDC  (you deposit 10,000 USDC, get 10,000 BFX-USD)
Day 30:  1 BFX-USD = 1.0040 USDC  (your 10,000 BFX-USD is now worth 10,040 USDC)
Day 90:  1 BFX-USD = 1.0120 USDC  (your 10,000 BFX-USD is now worth 10,120 USDC)
```

No claiming, no compounding, no harvesting. Yield is automatic.

## Withdrawing

1. Go to the Earn page
2. Click on your active pool position
3. Switch to **Withdraw**
4. Enter the amount of receipt tokens to redeem
5. Choose your output token
6. Confirm the withdrawal

**Cooldown:** There's a 5-minute cooldown after depositing before you can withdraw. After that, withdrawals are instant.

**No withdrawal fees.** You pay only the standard gas cost for the transaction.

## Risks to understand

<Warning>
Providing liquidity involves risk. Only deposit what you can afford to lose.
</Warning>

**Smart contract risk.** Funds are held in smart contracts. A bug or exploit could result in loss of funds. Contracts have not yet been formally audited.

**Oracle risk.** If an oracle reports an incorrect FX rate, the pool could execute trades at wrong prices, affecting LP value. Safety bounds limit but don't eliminate this risk.

**Utilization risk.** In extreme scenarios, if most pool liquidity is locked in active swaps, your withdrawal might be delayed until liquidity frees up.

**No impermanent loss (in the traditional sense).** Because pricing is oracle-based rather than pool-ratio-based, BlankFX pools don't suffer from the impermanent loss that AMM LPs experience. However, oracle mispricing could create a similar effect.
