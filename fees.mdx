# Fees

## Swap fees

BlankFX charges a spread on each swap. The spread is the difference between the oracle mid-rate and the rate you receive.

| Swap type | Typical spread |
|-----------|---------------|
| USD ↔ USD (e.g. USDC to USDT) | ~0.05% |
| Cross-currency (e.g. USDC to EURC) | ~0.10-0.30% |

Spreads are set by the protocol and may vary by pair and market conditions.

## Where fees go

100% of swap fees flow to liquidity providers. When you deposit into a pool, you earn your proportional share of all fees generated by swaps touching that pool.

The USD Pool earns fees from:
- All USD-to-USD swaps (USDC ↔ USDT ↔ RLUSD)
- The USD side of all cross-currency swaps (USDC → EURC, EURC → USDT, etc.)

This means USD Pool depositors earn from every trade on the protocol, regardless of currency pair.

## Gas fees

If you use gasless mode (default), you don't pay gas directly. The gas cost is included in the swap spread — you see one rate that covers everything.

If you submit transactions directly (advanced), you pay standard Ethereum gas fees.

## Deposit and withdrawal fees

There are no fees for depositing into or withdrawing from liquidity pools. You pay only the standard Ethereum gas cost for the transaction.

## No hidden fees

There are no platform fees, no withdrawal penalties, and no performance fees on yield earned. The spread on swaps is the only cost.
